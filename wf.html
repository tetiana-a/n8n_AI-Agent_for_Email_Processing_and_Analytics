Workflow Overview
This automated system handles customer support emails:

1. Fetch: Checks Gmail for new tickets every 15 minutes.
2. Context: Loads approved answers from Google Sheets.
3. AI Analysis: GPT-4o classifies intent and drafts a safe reply.
4. Safety: Complex cases are flagged for humans; safe ones become drafts.
